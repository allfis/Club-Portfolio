Who We Are

Bridging Academia
& Real Careers

"Let Passion Drive Your Profession"

KUET Career Club (KCC) is a career-oriented student club at Khulna University of Engineering and Technology. We exist to bridge the gap between academic excellence and real-world career readiness.

Our programs span career seminars, BCS preparation, study abroad guidance, technical workshops, and leadership development — all designed to give KUETians a decisive edge after graduation.
